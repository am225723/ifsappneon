6 F's Protocol Mastery Practice

Take a slow breath and begin by Finding the part that needs attention today.

Focus on the part with gentle awareness. Notice where it lives in or around the body.

Flesh it out by sensing its feelings, age, posture, image, or protective intention without forcing details.

Feel toward it from Self. If you notice fear, frustration, or urgency, ask those parts for space so curiosity can return.

Befriend the part by appreciating how it has tried to help you survive or cope.

Ask about its Fears. What is it afraid would happen if it stopped doing its job? Listen with patience and respect.

Close by letting the part know you will return, and write down one respectful next step.
