The Unburdening Ceremony

Find a comfortable position and invite your system to move slowly. Notice the parts that want to participate and the parts that may need more distance.

Ask protective parts for permission before approaching any burden. Thank them for how long they have worked to keep you safe.

When a wounded part feels ready, invite it to show you the belief, emotion, or burden it has been carrying. Stay with warmth, patience, and Self-energy.

Offer the part an image of release that feels right: light, water, earth, fire, wind, or another safe symbol. Let the part choose the pace.

After the release, ask what qualities the part would like to receive now. Invite calm, compassion, courage, clarity, or any needed resource to settle in.

Close by thanking every part involved and orienting back to the room.
