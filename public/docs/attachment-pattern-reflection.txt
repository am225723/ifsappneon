Attachment Pattern Reflection

Take a few breaths and bring to mind a recent relational moment that activated you.

Notice whether parts moved toward pursuit, withdrawal, caretaking, control, freezing, or self-protection.

Ask these parts what they were afraid would happen and what they were trying to protect.

Offer appreciation for their efforts, then invite Self-energy to bring calm and clarity.

Reflect on one secure response you can practice: naming a need, setting a boundary, asking for reassurance, or taking space kindly.

Close by reminding your system that attachment patterns can soften through repeated Self-led care.
