Personalized Wound Healing Action Plan

Settle in and bring to mind the wound pattern you are currently healing. Let this be information, not a label.

Ask your inner system which younger part most needs support with this wound today.

Notice what this part believes, needs, and fears. Let your adult Self respond with compassion and clarity.

Choose one healing action that is small enough to practice this week: reassurance, boundary setting, rest, play, journaling, or reaching out for support.

Imagine completing this action while staying connected to Self-energy.

Close by naming the next step and thanking your parts for helping you build a plan.
