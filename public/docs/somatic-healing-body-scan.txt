Somatic Healing Body Scan

Begin by feeling the surface beneath you. Let your eyes close or soften.

Scan from the crown of your head through your face, jaw, throat, shoulders, chest, belly, hips, legs, and feet.

Pause anywhere you notice tightness, numbness, warmth, heaviness, or emotion.

Ask gently: what might this sensation be holding, and how old does it feel?

Breathe warmth and compassion toward that area without trying to change it quickly.

Invite the body to take in one resource: support, space, strength, softness, or safety.

Close by moving fingers and toes and thanking your body for communicating.
