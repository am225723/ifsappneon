Befriending Your Inner Critic

Settle in and invite the inner critic to be noticed from a safe distance.

Instead of arguing with it, ask what it is trying to prevent or protect you from.

Notice the critic’s tone, age, fears, and burden. See if you can appreciate its protective intention, even if its method hurts.

Ask what it worries would happen if it stopped criticizing.

Offer the critic a new role that still protects you without shame: discernment, preparation, encouragement, or boundary awareness.

Close by thanking the critic for speaking and promising to return with curiosity.
