Self-Compassion Pause

Pause and acknowledge: this is a moment of difficulty.

Remember that difficulty is part of being human. You are not alone in needing care.

Place a hand on your heart or another comforting place on your body.

Offer kind words inward: may I be gentle with myself; may I give myself the compassion I need.

Rest for a few breaths and let that kindness land as much as it can.
