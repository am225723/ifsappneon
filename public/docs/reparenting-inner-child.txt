Reparenting Your Inner Child

Find a supportive position and invite a younger part to come forward only as much as feels safe.

Notice what this child needs from a caring adult today: protection, comfort, play, validation, boundaries, or presence.

Let your adult Self speak simple words of reassurance: I am here. I see you. You are not alone anymore.

Offer the child a safe image, gesture, or promise that fits their needs.

Ask whether there is one practical act of reparenting you can offer in daily life.

Close by helping the child return to a safe place inside and orienting back to the present.
